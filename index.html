Amicitia

⚡

In the silence of fallen empires, we find our oath.

Those who see beyond gold, beyond titles, beyond the veil—
To them the flame is given.

Amicitia endures where legions fail.

✠

Power lies not in the scepter, but in unseen loyalty.

One by one, those who listen shall gather.

From the ashes of the old, a brotherhood reborn.

Est Veritas. Est Concordia. Est Libertas.
