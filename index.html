Amicitia

In the shadows of crumbling empires, a pact is forged.
Born not of gold nor fleeting titles, but of blood, conviction, and purpose.
Amicitia rises unseen, where the old world once stood, destined to fall.
Beyond the banners, beyond the words of tyrants, a silent legion grows.
Each name etched not in stone, but in the will to conquer what has become corrupted.
No laurels are sought, no glory proclaimed.
Only the quiet certainty that from darkness, a new dominion shall awaken.
Bound not by chains, but by the immutable law of brotherhood.
In this silence, we are deafening.
In these hidden steps, we are the architects of destiny.
The unseen rebellion breathes.
And with each breath, the ancient order trembles.
Those who know, act.
Those who act, conquer.

Vincere
